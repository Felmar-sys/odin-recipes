Creamy Seasoned Avocado

Description

This is a simple, quick, and healthy way to prepare a ripe avocado. Prefect for a snack or a light breakfast

Ingredients

  • One or more ripe avocado (depending on your appetite).
  • Fresh lemons (Ratio: 1 lemon per avocado).
  • Sugar to taste.

Steps

  1. Peel the avocado and use a spoon to scoop it into a bowl.
  2. Pour the sugar over the avocado according to your preference.
  3. Squeeze the fresh lemon juice over the mixture.
  4. Cover the bowl and let it sit for 10 to 15 minutesuntil the sugar dissolves and the aroma enhances.
  5. Then, mash and beat the avocado until it reaches a smooth consistency.
  6. Server with bread or enjoy it just as it is.
