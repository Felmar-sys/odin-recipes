Easy Red Beans and Rice

Total Tome: 40 minutes | Servings 8

"Inexpensive, nutritious, easy enough for just about any night." - Kit, Allrecipes Member

Description

This easy red beans and rice recipe is a delicious version of a Louisiana classic. It can be prepared and cooked in 40 minutes! Prefect for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 (16 ounce) package turkey kielbasa, sliced diagonally into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans red beans, drained
  • 1 (16 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

    Photo by: Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

  2. Prepare the rice: Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.

    Photo by: Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

  3. Meanwhile, cook sausage in a large skillet over low heat for 5 minutes.

    Photo by: Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

  4. Stir in onion, green pepper, and garlic; saute until tender.

    Photo by: Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

  5. Add in beans and tomatoes with juice. Season with oregano, pepper, and salt.

    Photo by: Dotdash Meredith Food Studios

  6. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes. Serve over rice.

    Photo by: Dotdash Meredith Food Studios
